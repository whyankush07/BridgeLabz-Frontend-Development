Pseudo-elements

This paragraph demonstrates pseudo-elements. The ::before pseudo-element adds content before the element, and the ::after pseudo-element adds content after it. These are powerful tools for adding decorative elements without extra HTML markup.

CSS pseudo-elements let you style specific parts of an element's content.
